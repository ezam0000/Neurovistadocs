Revolutionizing Healthcare Diagnostics

NeuroVista is an AI-powered web application that assists healthcare professionals in diagnosing and treating a wide range of health conditions, leveraging advanced artificial intelligence to analyze patient data and generate multiple potential diagnostic and treatment options.

Key Features

  • Advanced AI analysis using AWS Comprehend Medical
  • Multi-solution generation for diagnoses
  • Intuitive interface for doctors to refine and select diagnoses
  • Integration with electronic health records (EHRs)
  • Multi-language support for global accessibility

Our Vision

NeuroVista aims to become an indispensable tool in modern healthcare, augmenting the capabilities of medical professionals and ultimately leading to improved patient care and outcomes. We strive to:

  • Reduce the likelihood of missed or delayed diagnoses
  • Provide doctors with a broader range of well-supported diagnostic possibilities
  • Streamline the process of considering and evaluating different treatment options
  • Improve patient outcomes through more accurate and timely diagnoses and treatments
  • Serve as a powerful learning tool for medical professionals
